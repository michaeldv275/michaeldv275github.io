About Us

Our Origin

Our company was established in January 1990 in the eastern part of Hikone City, Shiga Prefecture, near Lake Biwa with a Captial of 1.25 million yen. The meaning of Customate is Custom (Customer's preference) Mate (Companion) the name was decided in order to connect our hope and need to develop our customer's needs and preferences.
